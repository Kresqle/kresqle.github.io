Random text generator

What is it ?

Random text generator was one of my first projects when I started learning JavaScript. It's really simple : it takes words as input and mix them randomly to create a sentence.

How to use it ?

Everyting should be explicit but I'm gonna explain it just in case.

You need to type your list of subjects if the text area "subjects", each separated by a semicolon. Then do the same thing for verbs and complements.

Then, click on the "Train" button to train the program.

Finally, click on the "Generate" button to generate a random sentence. You can generate multiple sentences without having to retrain the program.
